Frequently Asked Questions
SharpPicks is a model-driven sports market intelligence platform covering NBA, WNBA, and MLB. Our four-model ensemble evaluates games daily and surfaces high-confidence picks based on closing line value, historical performance, and real-time market data. We publish a maximum of one pick per day, only when the model identifies a meaningful edge.
Visit app.sharppicks.ai to create an account or sign in. If you're having trouble logging in, try resetting your password. If the issue persists, contact us at support@sharppicks.ai.
SharpPicks uses a four-model ensemble with 56 features and walk-forward validation. Picks are only published when the model detects a minimum edge threshold, and every pick undergoes pre-tip revalidation. If the edge decays before tip-off, the pick is withdrawn. We track closing line value as the primary signal for model health.
Subscription management — including upgrades, cancellations, and billing history — is handled through your account at app.sharppicks.ai. If you need help with a billing issue, email support@sharppicks.ai.
Edge is the difference between our model's projected probability of an outcome and the probability implied by the market line. A higher edge percentage indicates the model sees greater value relative to the market. We only publish picks when this edge exceeds our minimum threshold.
